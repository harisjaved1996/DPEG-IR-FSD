# Launch Process Checklist — Grouped by Notes (Timing) Text

Tasks from the **"launch process"** sheet, grouped strictly by the **Notes (timing) text**, in the order each timing first appears in the sheet. Each distinct Notes value is its own group, with all matching tasks and their responsibilities listed underneath.

**Consolidation note:** Two trivial text variants were merged — *"1 days"/"1 day prior to the launch"* are treated as one group, and *"From launch upuntill fulfillment"/"up untill fulfillment of raise"* as one group (this pulls in the row that sits just after the "Upon full completion of all PPMs" task). All tasks, timings, and responsibilities are taken directly from the sheet.
